What are design solutions?

The design solution is used to generate end product specifications to produce product verification. This create's a user experience to make a blueprint for what is being made. A design solution is needed to ask and know why something has to be made.

This is an example of a design solution process which idenfities the input, output and design solution process. This diagram was found on the NASA website on design solutions

Some tips on design solutions are to use the right tools such as visualisation supplies for designers such as sticky notes and markers, define the research for the problem, determine all the requirements for the project, do the research, brainstorm the ideas and create a prototype as well as testing the product to achieve the best results.
