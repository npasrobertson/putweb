Product Testing

What is product testing?

Product testing is simply put just making sure that the product works. Each aspect will be tested to make sure it works before it is sold to a customer, this stops the customer from recieving a product that is faulty or is incomplete.(<p>).

When testing a product its best to make a test table to keep track of all test made and what the results are from doing the tests. The table should include what your doing for the test, what happend when it was tested e.g. error message, what should happen when tested and any evidence of the test. The evidence could be a screenshot, photo or video.
