About us:

Our company was originally designed and practiced at the end of 2020 and the end of 2021, practicing recipes and techniques while the world was in lockdown.

However, at the start of 2022, there was a store available to rent in Queensgate, and decided to turn this simple hobby, completed for enjoyment, into a full time job, which the employee's and I love.

After renovating the shop to our ideal image, we began advertising and trying new recipes and here we are, 5 months later, with a website, 100 customers (at least) daily, and a new food diner family.
